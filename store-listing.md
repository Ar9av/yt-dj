# Chrome Web Store Listing Information

## Basic Information
- **Extension Name**: YouTube Timestamp DJ
- **Short Description** (132 characters max):
  Assign timestamps to keyboard keys QWERTYUIOP and instantly jump to them while watching YouTube videos. Perfect for DJs and content creators!

- **Detailed Description**:
  YouTube Timestamp DJ revolutionizes how you navigate YouTube videos! Instead of manually scrubbing through long videos, assign timestamps to your keyboard keys QWERTYUIOP and jump instantly to any point with a single keypress.

  **Perfect for:**
  - DJs mixing tracks with precise cue points
  - Content creators reviewing specific segments
  - Students jumping between lecture sections
  - Anyone watching long-form content

  **Features:**
  - Assign timestamps to QWERTYUIOP keys (10 total)
  - One-click timestamp capture from current video position
  - Manual timestamp entry with flexible time formats (MM:SS, HH:MM:SS, seconds)
  - Timestamps save automatically per video
  - Smart keyboard detection (ignores input fields)
  - Works only on YouTube for privacy and performance

  **How to use:**
  1. Click the extension icon on any YouTube video
  2. Set timestamps by typing them or clicking "Set Current"
  3. Press QWERTYUIOP keys while watching to jump instantly
  4. Timestamps are saved and persist between sessions

  **Privacy:** This extension only works on YouTube and stores timestamp data locally on your device. No data is transmitted or collected.
